How the inputs work

Quick definitions to match DK optimizer behavior.

Min Unique

Minimum number of players that must be different between any two lineups. Higher = more variety, but can reduce feasibility.

Randomness

Adds a small random boost/penalty to projections so lineups aren’t identical. Higher = more lineup diversity, but slightly less “pure optimal.”
